You can apply for the Energy Bill Discount Scheme for ETIIs

You will need to provide evidence to support your application and information about your energy suppliers and meters so we can pass on the discount.

Evidence for your application

You will need to provide a letter signed by a named director or equivalent of your organisation or business that states:

  • all the information in this application is accurate to the best of their knowledge
  • your organisation or business is an ETII operating in one or more eligible sectors, providing relevant SIC codes
  • a minimum of 50% of revenue falls within the eligible sectors
  • a summary of why you think you meet the above criteria and what evidence you have relied upon to make that assessment

A list of eligible sectors is available on GOV.UK

You may also need to provide:

  • a letter signed by a chartered accountant or auditor confirming that you are in an eligible sector, and that 50% or more of your revenue is in an eligible sector
  • your most recent full set of end-of-year accounts - if you do not have these, upload evidence of company accounts over a minimum of 6 months
  • an income statement referring to the same time period as the end-of-year accounts
  • a breakdown schedule which separates activities between eligible and ineligible SIC codes
  • a sample of sales invoices and VAT returns

Information about your energy suppliers

You will need to tell us:

  • the name of your energy suppliers
  • your meter numbers for each supplier

If you pay your energy suppliers directly give details of all your energy suppliers and meter numbers.
