You can apply for the Energy Bill Discount Scheme for ETIIs

You will need to provide evidence to support your application and information about your energy suppliers and meters so we can pass on the discount.

You will receive a certificate of eligibility if your application is successful.

Evidence for your application

You will need to provide a letter signed by a named director or equivalent of your organisation or business that states:

  • all the information in this application is accurate to the best of their knowledge
  • your organisation or business is an ETII operating in one or more eligible sectors, providing relevant SIC codes
  • a minimum of 50% of revenue in financial year 2022-2023 falls within the eligible sectors (local authorities should state that 50% of activities fall within the eligible sectors)
  • a summary of how you think you meet the criteria and what evidence you have relied on to make that assessment

A list of eligible sectors is available on GOV.UK

You may also need to provide financial evidence such as:

  • your most recent full set of end-of-year accounts - if you do not have these, upload evidence of your most recent company accounts, covering a minimum period of the most recent 6 months
  • an Income Statement referring to the same time period as the accounts
  • a sample of 20 sales and purchase invoices, no older than 12 months from the date of the signed declaration, which demonstrates activity that falls within an eligible sector 
  • license or trade body membership details for regulated industries (if applicable)
  • floor plans of the relevant property that show that 50% or more of your activities are in an eligible sector (local authorities only)

To help with the processing of your application, you could also upload a letter signed by a chartered auditor or accountant. This should confirm that you are in an eligible sector, and that 50% or more of your sales, services or revenue are in an eligible sector.

Information about your energy suppliers

You will need to tell us:

  • the name of your energy suppliers
  • your meter numbers for each supplier

If you pay your energy suppliers directly give details of all your energy suppliers and meter numbers.
