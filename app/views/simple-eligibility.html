You can register for the Energy Bill Discount Scheme for ETIIs

You will need to provide evidence to support your registration and information about your energy suppliers and meters so we can pass on the discount.

Evidence for your registration

You will need to provide a letter signed by a named director or equivalent of your organisation or business that states:

  • all the information in this registration is accurate to the best of their knowledge
  • your organisation or business is an ETII operating in one or more eligible sectors, providing relevant SIC codes
  • a minimum of 50% of revenue falls within the eligible sectors
  • a summary of why you think you meet the above criteria and what evidence you have relied upon to make that assessment

A list of eligible sectors is available on GOV.UK

Information about your energy suppliers

You will need to tell us:

  • the name of your energy suppliers
  • your meter numbers for each supplier

If you pay your energy suppliers directly, give details of all your energy suppliers and meter numbers.

If you pay for your energy through an intermediary provider and do not know your suppliers or meter numbers, you will need to share your eligibility certificate with your intermediary provider.
