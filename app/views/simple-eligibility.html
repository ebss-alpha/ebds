You can register for the energy bill discount scheme

In order to register for this discount, you will need to provide:

  • A letter signed by your CFO / CEO confirming that 50% or more of your sales/services revenue fall into an eligible sector. A list of eligible sectors is available on GOV.UK

You will need to give your energy supplier names and associated meter numbers.

Any meter numbers that you do not have access to, for example if you pay through an intermediary, will need to applied for by the person responsible for those meters and they should pass the discount onto your business.
