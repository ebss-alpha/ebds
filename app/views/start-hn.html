Use this service if you are a heat network supplier in the UK who needs to register for the energy bills discount scheme support. Heat network suppliers must register for the scheme, even if their current contract prevents them from getting the discount.

Eligible heat networks 

A heat network may be eligible for the discount if it: 

  • is a heat network as defined in the Energy Bills Discount Scheme regulations
  • is supplied by a licenced energy supplier
  • supplies one or more domestic customers
  • uses gas or electricity for producing heat

All meter points supplying the energy centre for the heat network are eligible for support.

Heat suppliers must also register under the redress scheme, managed by the Energy Ombudsman. Learn more about the redress scheme.

You can only register for one heat network at a time.

What is not eligible

If you are a heat network supplier, the discount will not apply to:

  • gas used to generate electricity to export back to the grid, including if it has been stored first
  • gas or electricity made available to customers in the Republic of Ireland

Before you start 

You will need:  

  • contact details of the applicant (including email address and phone number)
  • name and address of the heat supplier
  • postcode of the primary energy centre
  • supplier name and meter numbers for eligible meters at that heat network
  • completed declaration document signed by one of the following:
    • a named director of the heat supplier  
    • (if not a company) a named person approved by the Secretary of State for the purpose of making such a declaration 
    • (if not a company) the heat supplier themself if a person

What you need to know

After you apply, your registration details and related evidence will be assessed to determine if you should receive the discount.

You’ll be told about this decision by email. You should get a response within X weeks.

If you’re eligible, a qualifying heat supplier certificate will be emailed to you. This will include information on steps you may need to take to get the discount.
