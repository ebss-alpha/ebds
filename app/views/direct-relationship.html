Do you pay energy suppliers directly for the energy you use?

This means that you get the energy bills directly from a supplier. You might have used a broker to set this up.

If you don't pay your supplier directly, you might pay through an intermediary such as:

  • a landlord
  • a a third party energy management company
  • the Crown Commercial Service
